About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Accounting at Pennsylvania State University. Before joining PSU, I worked at the PCAOB OERA.

My research interests mainly focus on financial reporting, Knightian uncertainty, and corporate resource allocation. I employ empirical methods using Python and Stata to investigate how uncertainty affects managerial estimates and fair value reporting.
